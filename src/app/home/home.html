Admin Home

To start using Elite Schedule, configure your League first. Once you select your league, you will configure Teams and Games for your league.

Locations are shared across leagues.

Helpful tips:

  • Use the Home tab to create helpful announcement for coaches/parents to stay up-to-date.
  • It's good to configure Locations early in the process since you'll need to assign Locations for each game.
  • After creating games, you can always go back and change the start time.
